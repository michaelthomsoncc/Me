How Our Technology Works

Our approach harnesses AI to create tailored rules for each new invoice format encountered. These rules define document structures by mapping spatial and logical relationships between static labels and headings and variable data items that you need to capture. This method adapts dynamically, ensuring flexibility across diverse layouts—whether multi-column tables, embedded images, or irregular sections. Every rule is rigorously reviewed and validated by human experts to guarantee it captures the correct data while eliminating false positives. If the AI missteps, our team can refine the rules or take full control in the most intricate cases. Should a document reveal unexpected variations—unseen in an initial sample—we detect the issue, adjust the rules, and rerun the process to ensure nothing slips through.

For genuinely missing data (e.g., fields absent from the PDF), we deploy an innovative enhancement layer. By integrating off-document data sources—like customer-supplied purchase order feeds or product catalogs—we enrich and validate the data. This allows us to flag discrepancies, such as when invoice data doesn’t align with a purchase order, all before the document reaches your system. From your perspective, it’s invisible—just a reliable, structured data feed into your ERP or accounting platform.

Streamlined Efficiency: Less Labor, More Precision

Unlike traditional methods that rely on annotating documents to train custom AI models—a time-consuming and resource-heavy process—our hybrid AI-human system delivers superior results with significantly less effort. By leveraging tailored, dynamically generated rules and human validation, we eliminate the need for extensive manual annotations. This means you avoid the repetitive, labor-intensive task of labeling countless document samples to train a model. Our process is approximately 5x less human effort compared to training custom models, as we focus on creating adaptable rules that evolve with each new document format, rather than requiring exhaustive upfront training. This efficiency doesn’t come at the cost of accuracy—our technology achieves ~100x fewer errors than generic AI models and ~10x fewer errors than custom-trained models, delivering a remarkable 99.9+% document-level accuracy (where a document is correct only if all fields are accurately extracted) and a guaranteed 100% character-level accuracy through AI and human-in-the-loop validation. By combining AI-driven rule creation with targeted human oversight and off-document data integration, we streamline workflows, reduce manual touchpoints, and deliver precise, actionable data with minimal effort.
